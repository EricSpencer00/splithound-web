Introduction

Welcome to FairShare. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the FairShare mobile application ("App").

By using FairShare, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use FairShare, you may provide:

  • Receipt Images: Photos of receipts you scan using the App's camera
  • Receipt Data: Parsed receipt information including items, prices, totals, merchant names, and dates
  • Contact Information: Names, phone numbers, or email addresses of friends you add to split bills with (if you use the contacts feature)
  • Payment Information: Payment links or identifiers for third-party payment services (e.g., Venmo usernames, PayPal links)
  • Custom Data: Any notes, tags, or custom information you add to receipts

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the App, features used, and general usage patterns (collected anonymously)
  • Technical Data: App version, crash reports (if you opt in), performance metrics

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Credit card numbers or banking information
  • Social Security numbers or government IDs
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Personal identifiable information beyond what you explicitly provide

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Primary Functions

  • Provide Core Services: Process receipts using OCR, calculate splits, generate payment links
  • Store Your Data: Save your receipts and splits locally on your device and optionally in your private iCloud storage
  • Sync Across Devices: If you enable cloud sync, synchronize your data across your devices using Apple CloudKit
  • Manage Subscriptions: Track your subscription status and entitlements through RevenueCat

Improvements and Support

  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns (anonymously) to improve features and user experience
  • Fix Issues: Diagnose and fix technical problems and bugs
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and support requests

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect our legal rights and prevent misuse

Data Storage and Processing

Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Receipt images
  • OCR-extracted data
  • Friend contacts and payment information
  • Split calculations and history

We do not have access to data stored locally on your device.

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored in your private Apple CloudKit database. This means:

  • Data is encrypted and stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure
  • Only you can access this data through your Apple ID
  • We cannot access, view, or retrieve your CloudKit data
  • Data is governed by Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time in the App settings

On-Device Processing

All OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and receipt processing happens entirely on your device. Receipt images are not uploaded to our servers for processing.

Third-Party Services

FairShare integrates with several third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy:

Apple CloudKit

  • Purpose: Cloud storage and synchronization (optional)
  • Data Stored: Your receipts, splits, and app data in your private CloudKit database
  • Privacy: Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Your Control: You can disable cloud sync in Settings

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, subscription status, purchase receipts
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Note: RevenueCat does not receive your receipt images or split data

Apple App Store

  • Purpose: Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Data Shared: Apple ID, purchase information
  • Privacy: Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

Payment Services (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, etc.)

  • Purpose: Generate payment request links
  • How It Works: The App generates standard payment links (URLs) that open these services
  • Data Shared: Only the information you include in the payment link (amount, note)
  • Important: We do not process payments or have access to your payment accounts
  • Privacy: Each service has its own privacy policy

Overview

SplitHound is an application that assists with receipt scanning and simple bill-splitting workflows. We intentionally minimize collection of personal data.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or payment credentials as part of the core app functionality.
  • Image processing (OCR) and model inference is performed on-device wherever possible; we do not send raw receipt images to our servers.

No server-side processing

This application is a frontend (on-device) tool. OCR, image processing, and model inference are performed on the user's device. We do not transmit receipt images, OCR output, or other user data to any server operated by the provider as part of normal app operation.

Analytics & purchases

We do not collect analytics or purchase data on our servers. Any in-app purchases are processed by the platform (for example, the Apple App Store or Google Play). The provider does not retain receipt images or raw OCR data on a server. The app developer may receive standard reporting from the app store (sales totals, aggregated reports) which are not tied to personal identifiers by the provider.

Crash reports and logs

The App does not send crash reports or error logs to the provider by default. If you enable optional crash reporting or diagnostics within the app (for example, via a setting), that data may be transmitted to a third-party crash-reporting provider; in that case the provider will disclose the provider name and the data transmitted and provide an option to opt out.

Local storage and cookies

The App stores data locally on your device (including drafts, parsed receipt data, and settings). If the App includes a web component, that component may use local storage or cookies within the webview. Local storage data remains on your device unless you explicitly export or sync it.

Contacts and syncing

The App may offer an optional feature to import or sync contacts from your device to make splitting bills easier (for example, assigning line items to people). This is an opt-in feature: the App will request the appropriate system permissions before accessing contacts. Any contacts imported for use in the App are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to or retained by the Provider's servers as part of normal operation.

If you explicitly export contacts or sync them to a third-party service (for example, to a cloud backup or an external integration), that export is performed by you and is outside the control of the Provider. Please ensure you have permission to export or share contact information and consult any applicable privacy rules in your jurisdiction.

Retention and security

Local drafts and receipt data are stored on the user's device unless the user explicitly exports or syncs them. We recommend users review and delete any sensitive content before sharing. We take reasonable technical measures to secure any server-side aggregated data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

GDPR and similar laws

This application is designed to avoid collection of Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and to perform processing on-device. If you export, sync, or otherwise send data to third-party services, you are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable laws such as GDPR. This privacy policy does not constitute legal advice. If you operate in the EU or process EU personal data, consult legal counsel to confirm compliance obligations.
